-   We say r.v. $$U$$ and r.v. $$V$$ are orthogonal if $$Cov(U,V) = 0$$ or $$\mathbb{E}[UV] = \mathbb{E}[U]\mathbb{E}[V]$$
-   If either have zero mean, then orthogonality means $$\mathbb{E}[UV] = 0$$ by itself
